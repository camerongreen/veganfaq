Human exceptionalism

The belief that there is an impassable divide between humankind and all other life

Unquestioned assumption underlying much thought in our society

Overlap with concept of speciesism

Part of the philosophical underpinnings of our society, which are largely ignored

Speciesism

"...a prejudice or bias in favour of the interests of members of one's own species and against those of members of other species" Peter Singer, Animal Liberation, 1975

Has come to have a wider connotation, such as the difference in treatment between pets and farm animals

For example, pigs have a greater level of intelligence than dogs. Despite this, pigs are allowed to be kept in conditions in which it would be illegal to keep a dog.
