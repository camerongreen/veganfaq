    Don't plants have feelings?

    • The line between plants and animals is blurry. Muscles, sea anemones, mushrooms, all sit in the middle.
    • Central nervous system and brain thought to be required to feel pain.
    • Most creatures we eat are herbivorous so we save more plants by consuming them directly.
